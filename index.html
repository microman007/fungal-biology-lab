Our Research

Our lab investigates the molecular and genetic mechanisms underlying fungal pathogenesis and symbiosis. We use plant genetic engineering to enhance crop resistance, stress tolerance, and productivity. Integrating genomics, proteomics, and biotechnology approaches, we aim to develop sustainable agricultural solutions for future challenges.
